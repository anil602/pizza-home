Chicago's Best Pizza Made from Scratch Daily

Above all, Home Run Inn is a proud, family-owned business -one with deep roots in the city of Chicago. In 1923, the original Home Run Inn location opcned as a small tavern on Chicago's South Side.

Founded by Mary and Vincent Grittani, the tavern received it's name one fateful day when a baseball from the neighbourhood park smashed through one of the tavern's windows, a home run for some young slugger on the sandlot.

In 1947, after that baseball made history, Mary and her son-in-law, Nick Perrin, crafted the iconic Chicago pizza recipe known today.
